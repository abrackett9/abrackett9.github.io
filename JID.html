J.I.D


J.I.D, whose real name is Destin Route, is a hip hop artist from Atlanta. He released his first mixtape in 2010 when he attending Hampton University with a full ride football scholarship. At Hampton University he met a rap duo named Earthgang and founded the musical group spillage village, which now has 8 members. J.I.D woud lose his football scholarship after an injury, and then would be kicked out of the university along with the members of Earthgang a week before graduation. J.I.D then started focusing on making music, he released a couple solo mixtapes and albums with Spillage village which got the attention of J Cole who signed J.I.D and Earthgang to his record label, Dreamville. J.I.D released his first studio album 3 weeks later, The Never Story, in 2017. The Never Story and his signing witha famous artist proppelled him from the underground to somewhat mainstream success. J.I.D released his 2nd studio album in 2018, Dicaprio 2, which cemented him as a promising new artist. He wouldn't release another album untill 2022 with The Never Story, but gained a lot of popularity through features and collaboration albums. The Forever Story was his most succesful project, peaking at number 12 on the billboard top 100 albums. J.I.D's musical style is characterized by his intricate wordplay, dynamic flow, and diverse range of influences, which include jazz, funk, soul, and trap music. J.I.D has cemented himself as one of raps most talented artists with his genre bending approach to his music.
